# Brief: "The family group chat is a horror movie and I am the final girl"

**Format:** 5-slide carousel
**Hook:** "37 unread. I will simply never open the app again."
**Visual tone:** Notification badges escalating like a horror body count. Red dot cinema.

---

## Concept

Every slide is the same home screen icon. The only thing that changes is the red notification badge number.
The joke is that the number itself becomes suspenseful — a slasher film pacing built entirely out of the iOS badge UI.
By slide 4 the badge is no longer a number, it's a threat. Slide 5 is the one safe door: the app.

---

## Slide Breakdown

### Slide 1 — The Opening Shot
**Visual:** Phone home screen. Messages icon. Badge: "3".
**Text overlay:** "Three. Fine. Normal. Manageable."
**Tone:** Establishing shot. Lull the audience.

### Slide 2 — The First Scream
**Visual:** Same home screen. Same icon. Badge now: "12".
**Text overlay:** "Someone started a thread. Without me. I can feel it."
**Tone:** First tension beat. The camera hasn't moved. Only the number.

### Slide 3 — Act Two
**Visual:** Same icon. Badge: "28". Slight red glow behind the badge (cinematic lighting).
**Text overlay:** "Aunt Linda is sharing an article. This will be discussed."
**Tone:** We've lost control of the situation.

### Slide 4 — The Jump Scare
**Visual:** Same icon. Badge: "37". Red glow intensified. The rest of the screen slightly darker.
**Text overlay:** "37 unread. I will simply never open the app again."
**Sub-text:** "This is now a haunting."
**Tone:** The punchline. Let it land. This is the slide people screenshot.

### Slide 5 — The Final Girl Survives
**Visual:** Text Your Mom notification sliding down over the home screen. Calm, warm color. "Hey — jump in on Day 1. We'll flag the important stuff. 💬"
**Text overlay:** "Text Your Mom is the one door that's not locked."
**Tone:** Relief. Warm. The horror movie ends.

---

## Creative Notes

- The phone home screen stays visually identical across slides 1–4. Only the badge number and the red glow escalate.
- Badge number progression (3 → 12 → 28 → 37) should feel like a heartbeat — not linear
- Slide 4 is the hero. The red glow is the only real visual flourish. Do not get busy.
- Resist cutting to actual chat content. The badge *is* the horror.
- Could pair with a low creaky-door SFX if posted as video — but works silent.
