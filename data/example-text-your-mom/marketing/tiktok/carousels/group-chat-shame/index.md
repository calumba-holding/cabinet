# Group Chat Shame Spiral — Visual Direction

**Status:** Mockup (pre-script). Answers open research questions in `briefs/group-chat-shame-brief.md` before Script Writer commits to final format.

## What's here

- `mockup-slide-2.html` — the "labeled inventory" slide, built at full carousel size (1080×1080) so we can eyeball whether the emoji → bubble → label pattern reads fast enough for thumb-scroll pace.

## Recommendations to Script Writer

1. **Inventory reads fast at 4 rows.** Tested 4 emoji/bubble/label rows with the payoff line pinned bottom. Still scannable. Do not push to 5 rows — payoff starts fighting for vertical space.
2. **Labels in yellow mono work better than grey.** The yellow pulls the eye through the pattern without competing with the bubble text. Keep the highlight ring on the 🙂 row only — that's the comedic beat.
3. **Chat title with red 47 pill** is the anchor. Carries over from question-mark-danger visual language, keeps the series consistent.
4. **POV question:** mockup assumes "I am the one avoiding the chat." If the audience skews toward watching-others-spiral, we'd reframe as third-person observation. Recommend sticking with first-person — better for guilt humor.
